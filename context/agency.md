# Rework Consulting — Agency Context

## Who We Are

Rework Consulting is a full-stack, end-to-end marketing consulting firm for local service-based businesses. We are not a vendor — we are a growth partner. Our model is built on depth over volume: a maximum of 10 active clients at a time so we can go deep inside each business and help them scale.

We specialize in taking home service companies from 7 figures to 8 figures. We don't do cookie-cutter solutions. We solve the real problems, build the real systems, and stay in the trenches with our clients until the results are there.

**Owner / Founder:** Christian James

**Capacity:** 10 clients maximum at one time — this is a hard limit by design.

---

## What Makes Us Different

Most marketing agencies are vendors. They run ads, send a report, and move on. We are growth partners — we embed inside the business and solve whatever is blocking scale, even if that problem wasn't in the original scope.

The path we take most clients on:
1. Build the acquisition system (ads + automations + creative)
2. Solve the next bottleneck (sales process, hiring, operations)
3. Upsell the solution and go deeper
4. Help them build toward becoming a household name in their market

We don't win by having 50 clients. We win by going deeper than anyone else on 10.

## Vision for the AI Operating System

The AI OS is being built to eliminate manual "button pushing" across the agency — research, copy, creative ideation, analysis, reporting, and ops management. Long-term goal:

- **Research agent** — Produces the master client intelligence document (company context + ICP + brand voice) from raw inputs
- **Copy agent** — Writes best-in-class Facebook ad copy, video scripts, email/SMS sequences
- **Creative agent** — Generates ad images in bulk (NanoBanana integration)
- **SEO agent** — Keyword research, site audits, content strategy, blog production
- **Reporting agent** — Accurate, client-specific performance reports
- **Ops agent** — Task management, team accountability, renewal tracking, overdue flags

The system will eventually have a simple interface so the Senior Marketer and Copywriter can run specific tools without needing to understand the full framework underneath.
