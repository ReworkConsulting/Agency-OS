[HOOK]
You don't have to pay for it all today.

[BODY]
We offer zero percent financing on jobs over $1,000. That means you get it fixed now — and pay it off over time at zero interest.

Most of our customers are shocked at how low the monthly payment comes out.

Licensed, local, and we've been doing this for years in [city].

[CTA]
See what your monthly payment looks like. Call us.

---
STYLE NOTES: Leads with the financing offer immediately — no warmup. This works because the audience is people who want the service but are hesitating on cost. The "shocked at how low" line creates curiosity about the payment amount without stating it (they'll have to call to find out). CTA frames the call as a calculation exercise, not a sales pitch — removes fear of commitment.
